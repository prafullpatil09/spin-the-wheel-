About SpinDecide

Welcome to SpinDecide, your number one source for fair and transparent random decision making.

Founded in 2026, SpinDecide was created to solve a simple problem: Decision Fatigue. Whether you are a teacher picking a student, a gamer choosing a loadout, or a friend group deciding on dinner, we provide a mathematically fair way to "let go" and let the wheel decide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a clean, ad-supported, and high-performance utility tool that works on every device without requiring a login or personal data collection.

How it Works

Our wheel uses a Pseudo-Random Number Generator (PRNG) combined with CSS3 transitions and HTML5 Canvas to ensure that every spin is unique and unweighted. We believe in transparency and simplicity.
